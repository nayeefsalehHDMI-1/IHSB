About IHSB

Established in 1996 • A Legacy of Excellence

International Hope School Bangladesh (IHSB) is a leading English-medium school offering the Cambridge curriculum from Toddler to A-Level. With multiple campuses in Dhaka and Chattogram, we are committed to academic excellence and the holistic development of every child.

Our Vision

To nurture confident, compassionate, and competent global citizens who will lead with integrity and innovation.

  • Uttara Senior Section
  • Gulshan Campus
